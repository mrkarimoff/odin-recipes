Shashlik

Description

Shashlik, or shashlyk Pashto: شاشلک is a dish of skewered and grilled cubes of meat, similar to or synonymous with shish kebab. It is known traditionally, by various other names in the Caucasus and Central Asia,[2][3] and from the 19th century became popular as shashlik across much of the Russian Empire and nowaday in Russian Federation and CIS
